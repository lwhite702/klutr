# Muse

Muse is your creative remix engine that takes two ideas and blends them into something new and unexpected. Use Muse to discover connections between seemingly unrelated thoughts.

## How It Works

1. **Enter Idea A**: Type your first idea or note
2. **Enter Idea B**: Type your second idea or note
3. **Get Remix**: Muse combines both ideas into a creative insight

## Use Cases

- **Connect Disparate Thoughts**: Find relationships between unrelated notes
- **Generate New Ideas**: Use Muse to spark creative thinking
- **Explore Possibilities**: See what emerges when you combine concepts
- **Break Through Blocks**: Get unstuck by remixing existing ideas

## Example Combinations

- **Idea A**: "Morning routines boost productivity"
- **Idea B**: "Creative work requires unstructured time"
- **Muse Remix**: A novel insight about balancing structure and spontaneity

## Best Practices

- **Diverse Ideas**: Combine ideas from different domains for interesting results
- **Clear Concepts**: Well-defined ideas produce better remixes
- **Iterate**: Try different combinations to explore various angles

## Tips

- Use Muse when you feel stuck or need fresh perspectives
- Combine ideas from different time periods or contexts
- Save interesting remixes as new notes for further exploration

_Muse helps you see connections you never noticed and generate insights from the intersection of your ideas._
